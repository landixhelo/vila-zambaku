About Vila

Vila Zambaku, a property with a garden and a terrace, is set in Ksamil, 600 metres from Bora Bora Beach, 700 metres from Ksamil Beach 7, as well as 2.8 km from Butrint National Park. The air-conditioned accommodation is 400 metres from Ksamil Beach, and guests benefit from private parking available on site and free WiFi.

Ksamil Beach 7 is 400 metres from the accommodation, while Butrint National Park is 2.9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 93 km from Oxhaku Guest House.
